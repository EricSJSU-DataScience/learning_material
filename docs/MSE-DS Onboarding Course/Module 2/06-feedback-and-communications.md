# Program Feedback and Communications

Penn Engineering Online values your feedback and strives to create an engaging and welcoming learning experience. This page outlines how to communicate your concerns and how the program communicates with you.

---

## Feedback Channels

We want to ensure your concerns and opinions are captured and addressed through the correct channels.

### Where to Send Feedback

**Program Email**  
[pennengonline@seas.upenn.edu](mailto:pennengonline@seas.upenn.edu)
- General feedback, issues, and personal concerns
- [Contact information in Student Knowledge Base](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/student-knowledge-base/connect-with-student-support/)

**Student Success Office Hours**  
[Connect with Student Support](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/student-knowledge-base/connect-with-student-support/)
- Discuss feedback, problems, and concerns directly with staff
- Available for one-on-one consultation

**End of Semester Course Evaluations**  
- Feedback specific to individual courses
- Topics: Instructors, TAs, content, recommendations
- Completed at the end of each semester

**Annual Penn Engineering Online Program Survey**  
- Conducted every fall
- Program-wide feedback opportunity

### Important Note

**Ed Discussion is NOT appropriate** for course or program-level feedback, even if posted privately. Use the channels listed above instead.

---

## Program Communications

### Email Announcements

The program sends email announcements to your Penn email address while you are enrolled. 

**Important information includes:**
- Semester schedules
- Critical deadlines
- Course registration details

**Your responsibility:** Read program announcements and take action when needed.

### Email Address and Directory

**Finding your current email address:**
- Look up your information in the [Penn Directory](https://directory.upenn.edu/)
- Confirm your email address is correct and current

**Updating your email address:**
- If you need to update your email, [contact CETS through their official contact page](https://cets.seas.upenn.edu/contact-us/)

**Important:** Emails are sent to the address listed in the Penn Directory. Program announcements are your responsibility to track and act upon.

### Accessing Forms and Documents

When receiving documents or forms from Penn Engineering Online program staff:

- **You must be logged in to your SEAS/PenKey account** to access forms
- Some content may be restricted to authenticated users only

---

## Email Correspondence Guidelines

### Include Your Penn ID

**Include your Penn ID number in all email correspondence** with the program and university.

This helps ensure:
- Proper identification of your account
- Faster processing of requests
- Clear documentation of communications

### Leave of Absence and Deferral

**If you are on Leave of Absence (LOA) or Deferral:**
- You will receive important program information via the **personal email address** listed in your application
- Regular Penn email may not be monitored during these periods

---

## Key Points

- Use program email for feedback, issues, and personal concerns
- End-of-semester evaluations capture course-specific feedback
- Annual program surveys occur every fall
- Ed Discussion is NOT for program or course feedback
- You are responsible for reading and acting on program email announcements
- Verify your email address in Penn Directory; update through CETS if needed
- Include your Penn ID in all university and program correspondence
- Access to forms and documents requires SEAS/PenKey login
- If on LOA or deferral, program communications go to your personal email address
