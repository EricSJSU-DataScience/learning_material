# MSE-DS Module 2: Academics and Course Selection

This module provides an overview of the MSE-DS Online curriculum requirements, feedback channels, and academic options. You will learn about the online course experience. Additionally, you will find course descriptions and can plan out your schedule to determine your goals and timeline for degree completion.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, you will be able to:

- Identify the required and elective courses that comprise the MSE-DS Online program
- Describe what the online course experience is like
- Recognize program requirements and registration policies/procedures
- Select the courses you will take in your first semester

---

## What You'll Learn

**Curriculum Overview**
Understand the structure of the MSE-DS program, including required courses, electives, and specialization options. Learn how the courses build on each other and connect to your career goals.

**Online Course Experience**
Discover what it's like to take courses in an online environment. Learn about course delivery methods, interaction with faculty and peers, and support resources available to you.

**Program Requirements**
Familiarize yourself with degree requirements, credit hours, registration policies, and procedures. Understand academic policies and timelines for degree completion.

**Course Planning**
Develop your course plan for the first semester and beyond. Learn how to select courses that align with your goals and manage your course load while balancing work and life.

---

## Key Points

- The MSE-DS program has a structured curriculum with required and elective courses
- Online courses offer flexibility while maintaining academic rigor and community
- Understanding program requirements and policies is essential for timely degree completion
- Strategic course selection early on helps you progress toward your goals
- Support resources are available throughout your academic journey
