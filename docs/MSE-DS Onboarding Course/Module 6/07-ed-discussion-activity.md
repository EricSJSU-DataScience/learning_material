# Ed Discussion Activity

## Get started with Ed Discussion!

### Steps to Complete This Activity

1. Access the Onboarding Ed Discussion Board by selecting it in the left sidebar.

2. Explore Ed Discussion and respond to the posted question or create your own post:

**Discussion Prompt:**

*If you've taken online classes in the past, what strategies did you find helpful for mastering the content? What other strategies or time management tools have you developed that you plan to bring to your studies?*

---

## Important Note

Each Penn Engineering Online course has its own Ed Discussion Board. You will receive information on how to connect to the Ed Discussion forum within each individual course.
