# Module 6 Description

In this module, you will learn best practices and strategies for studying and managing your time as an online student. It will allow you the opportunity to reflect on your strengths and areas of need when it comes to online learning. There will also be an opportunity to learn how Discussion Forums are used in your courses.

Learning online can present unique opportunities and challenges - particularly if you have not been a student in a few years! The following module includes resources as a means of introducing and/or refreshing you to some best practices in time management, goal setting, etc. Many of these strategies, even if they may seem like common sense, can have a tremendous impact on your success in a rigorous online degree program!

---

## Learning Objectives

- Identify best practices for studying and managing time as an online student
- Evaluate your academic strengths and needs in an online learning environment
- Describe how Discussion Forums are used in your courses
