## Key Takeaways on Time Management

- Review your syllabus and the course outline as soon as you receive it and add all key dates to a calendar or planning tool that you check daily.

- Check in with your course at the start of each week and make sure you understand all deadlines, expectations, and how you are being assessed for that week. If you are not sure, clarify with your instructor or TA.

- Dedicate regular times each week for completing course activities. If you have a tight schedule, block these times in your calendar.

- Track the time you spend on regular activities for your courses, such as watching lectures, participating in discussions, and completing assignments to develop better estimates about how much time you need to dedicate and when you need to make time available.

- Plan to start major assignments and group projects early.

- For group assignments that require you to collaborate with others: assign clear roles, set intermediate deadlines, and clarify how you will communicate with one another to make sure the work can be completed on time.

- Ask for help as early as possible.
