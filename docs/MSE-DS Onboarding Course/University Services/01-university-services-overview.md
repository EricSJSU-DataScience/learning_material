# University Services Overview

As a Penn student, you have access to a wide range of University-level support services which are primarily virtual. The following section will outline these services. You can find a quick reference overview of university-level [student support services](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/degrees/student-services/) on our website.

## PennCard

New Penn Engineering Online students will be mailed a Non-Photo PennCard. We estimate that you will receive this after your first semester.

If you would like to come to campus to pick up a photo PennCard, you must contact Penn Engineering Online beforehand to verify your student status. Once verified, you must call the [PennCard Center](http://cms.business-services.upenn.edu/penncard/the-penncard-center/hours-and-location.html) ahead of your visit to inquire about the steps for obtaining a photo **PennCard** from the [PennCard Center](http://cms.business-services.upenn.edu/penncard/the-penncard-center/hours-and-location.html).
