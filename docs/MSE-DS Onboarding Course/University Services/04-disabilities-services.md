# Disabilities Services

![alt text](04_image1.png)

Disabilities Services serves as a resource for Penn students with disabilities. If you have a disability and wish to self-identify, find the guide to begin the registration process [here](https://weingartencenter.universitylife.upenn.edu/getting-started/).

Completion of this form is **voluntary** and completion or non-completion will not adversely affect participation in educational programs and activities. The information collected in this form is **confidential** and will be shared only with appropriate University officials to assure compliance with Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 and the Americans with Disabilities Act as Amended 2008.

Submission of this form is the **first step** in the self-identification process. In addition, you will need to submit documentation of your disability. For more information see [Documentation Guidelines](https://wlrc.vpul.upenn.edu/getting-started/).

You must schedule a meeting (via email or phone) as part of the collaborative process in determining your eligibility for accommodations. Penn Engineering Online students with disability accommodations should **schedule a meeting before courses start**.

Disability Services will provide you with a determination in writing via email of your eligibility for accommodations after they have received all necessary documentation and you have participated in their collaborative process. For more information visit the [Disability Services website](https://weingartencenter.universitylife.upenn.edu/disability-services/).

Students with disability accommodations need to schedule meetings to determine eligibility for accommodations each semester, so please set a reminder for yourself to contact Disability Services each semester.
