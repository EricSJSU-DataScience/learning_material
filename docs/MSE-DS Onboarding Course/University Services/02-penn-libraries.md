# Penn Libraries

Hi, welcome to Penn Libraries. Even though you may not be on campus, we're your library. And we have many materials and services to offer you, from journal articles and newspapers to eBooks and streaming video. You have complete access at no cost to all our online subscriptions. Just go to the library's website, search for the publication in the search box, and when you click on the access link to materials we subscribe to, you just put in your PennKey and password.

You have access to other materials in several ways. We can send PDFs of book chapters and articles directly to you. And if you're in the United States, we can also send you physical books by mail. To use the Scan and Deliver service, or have books mailed, you need to sign in with your PennKey on the library's website.

Then when you look at a book or record in the catalog, there'll be options for what you can do, like scan and deliver or mail books. And if Penn doesn't have the articles you're looking for, you can get them sent to you from other libraries across the world through interlibrary loan. To learn more about these services, go to [guides.library.upenn.edu/resourcesharing](https://guides.library.upenn.edu/resourcesharing).

You can always ask us if you have questions. If you're ever on campus, you can take advantage of our study spaces, 3D and letterpress printers and our programming. But even if you never set foot in Philadelphia, you can still work with our librarians. Your program has a librarian assigned to it. This person has expertise in your field and is committed to and excited by working with students. You can consult your librarian about assignments or about research your conducting. We work with remote students by email, phone and videoconferencing. Just contact your librarian for an appointment or to ask a question.

You can also get assistance through chat. We're excited to have you at Penn and hope you make use of the materials and expertise we have to offer at Penn libraries. For more information, check out our guide for online students at [guides.library.upenn.edu/onlinestudents](https://guides.library.upenn.edu/onlinestudents).
