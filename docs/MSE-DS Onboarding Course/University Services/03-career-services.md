## Next Step: Penn Engineering Career Course

Now that you are a Penn student, you have access to a wealth of professional development opportunities. The [Next Step: Penn Engineering Career Course](https://canvas.upenn.edu/enroll/D4RJX8) is your starting point for building a successful and intentional career path. This self-paced Canvas course from Penn Engineering Career Services is designed to help you:

- Develop a personalized approach to exploring career options based on your goals, interests, and skills
- Engage with essential resources for career planning, including application materials, professional branding, job and internship search strategies, and networking

Completing this course will empower you to take a more strategic, informed, and confident approach to your career development. Once you complete Modules 1-3, you will unlock access to [1:1 career advising appointments](https://gradadmissions.seas.upenn.edu/manage/login?realm=&r=/portal/career_advising) with a member of our Career Services team.

## Career Advising Sessions

In your advising session, you can focus on topics such as:

- Mapping your career goals
- Writing a great resume and cover letter
- Job-search strategies
- Interviewing skills
- Networking skills
- Professional communication
- Building a strong personal brand

Start with the course to build a strong foundation, then meet with us to take the next step in your career journey.
