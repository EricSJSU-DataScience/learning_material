# Wellness at Penn and the Office of Student Health and Counseling

[Wellness at Penn](https://www.vpul.upenn.edu/caps/) houses the Office of Student Health and Counseling for the University of Pennsylvania designed to promote health and wellness throughout the University. Penn Engineering Online students can call any time of night or day at 215-898-7021 for information, support, to schedule an appointment, to speak with an on-call provider, and also to receive referral services for mental health providers in their area.

**Note: If you are experiencing an emergency, please contact local first responders.**

Wellness at Penn also has great tools to help students with mindfulness and self-care. Links to these tools are copied below and we strongly encourage students to bookmark these to keep in their own tool kit. You may also visit the [Student Knowledge Base](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/student-knowledge-base/) to access all Wellness at Penn resources.

1. [Explore mindfulness and self-care tools from Penn Wellness](https://wellness.upenn.edu/wellbeing-initiatives/mindfulness-and-self-care-tools)

2. [Learn how to care for yourself and others during stress, distress, and crisis](https://wellness.upenn.edu/counseling/caring-yourself-and-others-stress-distress-and-crisis)
