# MSE-DS Module 4: Tuition, Fees, and Financial Aid

This module provides information about tuition and fees for Penn Engineering Online courses, financial aid policies, and tools to view and manage your bill. You will learn how to navigate billing platforms and understand when and how to pay your bill.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, you will be able to:

- Identify the tuition and fees for Penn Engineering Online courses for this academic year
- Recognize when to pay your bill and when your bill is generated
- Gain an understanding of how to pay your bill through PennPay
- Locate where to get help to pay your bill
- Determine the best ways for you to pay your bill

---

## What You'll Learn

**Tuition and Fees Overview**  
Understand the cost structure of Penn Engineering Online programs, including per-course fees, comprehensive fees, and any additional charges. Learn how costs vary based on enrollment and course load.

**Billing Timeline and Policies**  
Discover when bills are generated throughout the semester, payment due dates, and important deadlines for financial obligations. Understand late payment consequences and payment options.

**Billing Platforms and Tools**  
Learn how to access your bill through Path@Penn and other systems. Understand how to view itemized charges, payment history, and account balance.

**PennPay Payment System**  
Get a detailed walkthrough of how to use PennPay, Penn's secure payment platform, to pay your tuition and fees online. Learn about payment methods and processing times.

**Financial Aid and Funding**  
Understand your financial aid package, how aid is applied to your bill, and important financial aid policies. Learn how to appeal financial aid decisions if needed.

**Support Resources**  
Identify the Student Financial Services office and other resources available to help you understand your bill, explore payment options, and address financial concerns.

---

## Key Points

- Tuition costs are based on course enrollment and course load
- Bills are generated on specific dates throughout the semester
- PennPay is the secure, preferred method for paying your bill online
- Multiple payment options are available to meet your needs
- Student Financial Services staff are available to answer questions and provide guidance
- Understanding your billing timeline helps you plan and budget effectively
- Financial aid can help offset tuition costs; learn how it applies to your bill
