# Billing

## Payment Options

You can pay your student bill through one of four methods of payment:

- Online through [Penn.Pay](https://srfs.upenn.edu/billing-payment/pennpay)
- Through the Mail
- In Person
- International Banks - By [Wire Transfer](https://srfs.upenn.edu/billing-payment/wire-transfer)

Please see the Student Financial Services website for more information on how to [pay your bill](https://srfs.upenn.edu/billing-payment/paying-your-bill).

---

## Billing Schedule

You can find the current billing schedule on the [SRFS website](https://srfs.upenn.edu/billing-payment/billing-schedule). Your billing statement will be issued according to the 'E-bill Sent' date, AFTER you register for courses. The bill due date is listed in your statement.

---

## Payment Plans

Payment plans are available for all semesters and you can review important deadlines on the [Payment Plan webpage](https://srfs.upenn.edu/billing-payment/penn-payment-plan).
