# MSE-DS Congratulations! What's Next?

Congratulations on completing the MSE-DS Online Onboarding Course! You are now officially ready to begin the first course in your program.

---

## What's Next?

- Going forward, you can use the Penn Engineering Online [Student Knowledge Base](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/student-knowledge-base/) to navigate the resources you will need as an MSE-DS Online student.

- Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need technical, academic, or university support. We are here to help. Communication outlets are outlined in the Support module.

- Something to keep in mind! MSE-DS Online students have the option to complete an Online Graduate Certificate. Learn more about the Online Graduate Certificate program [here](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/lifelong-learning/online-graduate-certificates/).

- [Download Word, PowerPoint, Excel, OneNote, Outlook, Teams, and more!](https://www.isc.upenn.edu/how-to/penno365-office-365-proplus) PennO365 provides full-time Penn students with access to Microsoft Office 365 ProPlus services and applications including the [University Client VPN](https://www.isc.upenn.edu/university-client-vpn).
