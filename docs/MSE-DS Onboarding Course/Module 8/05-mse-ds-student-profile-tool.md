# MSE-DS Student Profile Tool

We invite all incoming students to complete a Student Profile Tool that allows us to learn more about your background, motivations, and goals for the program.

## The Student Profile Tool includes two parts:

1. **Profile Questionnaire [5-10 minutes]:** This brief profile questionnaire is your opportunity to tell us more about yourself and your anticipated challenges in the program. We use this information to inform the types of support we develop for you and your classmates. Some of this information may have been contained in your application, but please provide the most up-to-date information available for the questions below.

2. **Career Questionnaire [5-10 minutes]:** This brief questionnaire will help us to understand your career goals and develop resources to better support you.

Completing the Student Profile Tool should take no longer than 20-30 minutes.

[Click here to access the Student Profile Tool](https://upenn.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_bQ2OgQ6yMnYjjxk).
