# Module 8: Support Resources

In this module, you will learn about support resources that are available to you, including course-level, student advising support, and university-level support. You will also learn about Advising Office Hours and Zoom and how they are used in your courses. For a complete list of all student support services discussed below, please visit the [Student Knowledge Base](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/student-knowledge-base/).

---

## Learning Objectives

- Identify how to receive support from various university resources
- Describe how to use Zoom and how it will be used in your courses
