# Path@Penn: Your Portal to Academic and Financial Information

[Path@Penn](https://path.at.upenn.edu/) is the University of Pennsylvania's comprehensive portal for managing your academic, financial, and student profile information. This is an essential tool you'll use throughout your studies at Penn.

---

## What is Path@Penn?

Path@Penn is your centralized hub for accessing and managing all academic and administrative information related to your Penn experience. You can access it anytime to check your records, manage finances, and plan your courses.

---

## Path@Penn Features

### Academic Records

- **Academic records and transcript orders** - View your grades, GPA, and transcript history
- **Course registration and schedule planning tools** - Register for courses and plan your schedule
- Degree audit and progress tracking

### Financial Information

- **Student billing and payment information** - View charges, invoices, and account balance
- **Financial aid application status and awards** - Check FAFSA status and aid packages
- **Student loan application status, disbursements, and loan history** - Track loan information

### Student Profile

- **Updating your emergency contacts and address information** - Keep your contact details current
- **Privacy settings for the release of academic and financial information** - Control information disclosure
- **Direct Deposit enrollment for student refunds** - Set up automatic deposit for refunds

---

## Important: Keep Your Information Current

**Throughout your entire program**, it is critical that you:

### Update Your "Learning From" Address

Keep your address information current in Path@Penn. This ensures:
- Important mail reaches you
- University communications are delivered correctly
- Your official records are accurate

### Maintain Emergency Contact Information

Ensure your emergency contact information is always up-to-date. This is essential for:
- University safety communications
- Administrative notifications
- Personal emergencies

### How to Update Your Profile

Make updates in the **"My Profile" section** of Path@Penn:

1. Log in to [Path@Penn](https://path.at.upenn.edu/)
2. Navigate to "My Profile"
3. Update your address and emergency contact information
4. Save your changes

---

## Course Registration Through Path@Penn

Path@Penn's course registration and schedule planning tools are your primary method for:
- Registering for courses each semester
- Viewing course availability and waitlists
- Planning your academic schedule
- Tracking your degree progress

**Note:** Detailed course registration instructions and deadlines are covered in the next sections of Module 3.

---

## Key Points

- Path@Penn is your comprehensive portal for academic, financial, and administrative information
- Use Path@Penn to register for courses each semester
- Keep your address and emergency contact information current in your "My Profile" section
- Access Path@Penn at any time to check grades, financial aid, billing, and course registration
- Your profile information affects official university communications and degree records
- Direct Deposit can be set up through Path@Penn for student refunds
- Academic privacy settings control information release to third parties
