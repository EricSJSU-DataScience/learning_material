# Penn Engineering Graduate Programs: Official Transcript Policy

All newly matriculating students must supply official transcripts or NACES evaluations for all colleges and universities attended.

Once you accept your offer of admission, please arrange for your official transcripts and/or NACES evaluations to be sent directly to Graduate Admissions as soon as possible.

Failure to do so will result in a hold on your registration and could ultimately result in your offer of admission being withdrawn.

---

## Due Dates

### Online Programs

All transcripts and/or evaluations are due **by the course drop deadline**.

See the [Penn Engineering Online Academic Calendar](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/degrees/student-services/academic-calendar/) for specific start dates.

---

## What is Required?

**Institutions in the United States:** We require your final official transcript showing completion of coursework required for the degree, degree earned, and the date of degree conferral. Transcripts should be submitted via a secure electronic service, such as Parchment.

**Institutions outside the United States:** For each university or college you've attended outside the U.S., we require that you contact a member of the National Association of Credential Evaluation Services (NACES) to have a course-by-course evaluation of your non-U.S. transcript(s) sent directly from the NACES member to Penn Engineering Graduate Admissions. This evaluation should include course titles, grades received, GPA, and U.S. degree equivalency.

While Penn Engineering accepts course-by-course evaluations from any NACES member, many of our applicants have been successful using:

- [Educational Perspectives](https://www.edperspective.org/)
- [Educational Credential Evaluators](https://www.ece.org/) (ECE)
- [International Education Evaluations](https://myiee.org/university/penn-engineering-graduate-and-professional-studies) (IEE)
- [SpanTran](https://spanside.my.salesforce-sites.com/SpantranApplication?Id=04eff67d-4e0b-4434-9797-76ead23b76d5)
- [World Education Services](https://www.wes.org/) (WES)

## Steps for Submitting Official Documents

### For U.S. Institutions

1. Contact all schools and universities you attended (via their website or by telephone) to request a transcript.

2. Ensure the transcript shows:
   - Completion of coursework required for the degree
   - Degree earned
   - Date of degree conferral
   - If still in final semester: wait until degree is conferred

3. Request official transcript be sent to:
   - **University of Pennsylvania**
   - **Dept: SEAS Graduate Admissions (Engineering)**

4. If the school cannot send electronically, provide this mailing address:
   - University of Pennsylvania SEAS Graduate Admissions
   - 3600 Market St, SUITE 240
   - Philadelphia, PA 19104

5. Once transcripts are received and verified, admissions will indicate the requirement has been met on the application checklist status page. Check your [admissions account portal](https://gradadmissions.seas.upenn.edu/apply/status) for status updates.

**IMPORTANT:** Provide all official transcripts for all schools attended.

### For Non-U.S. Institutions

1. Contact a NACES member as soon as possible to have a course-by-course evaluation of your non-U.S. transcript(s).

2. Follow the individual NACES member's instructions for submission.

3. Ensure the evaluation includes:
   - All courses
   - All grades
   - Degree conferred
   - If still in final semester: wait until degree is conferred

4. Request that the completed course-by-course evaluation be sent to:
   - **University of Pennsylvania**
   - **Dept: SEAS Graduate Admissions (Engineering)**

**IMPORTANT:** Provide NACES evaluations for all non-USA schools attended.

## Deadlines and Verification Process

**Submission Deadline:** All official transcripts and NACES evaluations are due **by the course drop deadline**. See the [Academic Calendar](https://online.seas.upenn.edu/degrees/student-services/academic-calendar/).

**Verification Timeline:**
- Verification begins AFTER the specified due date
- Can take up to 2 weeks after the due date for verification

**Non-Compliance:**
- Applicants who do not submit ALL transcripts and/or NACES evaluations will be placed on registration hold approximately 2 weeks after program start
- **You cannot continue in the program unless you satisfy the Official Transcript requirement**

---

## Transcript Verification Discrepancy

**Important:** If there are discrepancies between your self-reported academic work and official records:
- Your offer of admission may be withdrawn
- You may be removed from the program

Any student who fails to complete the official document verification process may have their offer of admission withdrawn and may be removed from the program.

---

## Key Points to Remember

- Submit transcripts **as soon as possible** after accepting your admission offer
- ALL transcripts from ALL schools must be submitted (U.S. and international)
- International transcripts require NACES course-by-course evaluations
- Deadline: by the course drop deadline
- Non-submission results in registration hold and potential removal from program
- Degree must be conferred before program start
- Keep verification status in your admissions portal
