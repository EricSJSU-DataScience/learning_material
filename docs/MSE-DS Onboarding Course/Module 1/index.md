# Module 1: Welcome!

This module introduces you to Penn Engineering Online, the MSE-DS program community, and essential tools for your success. You'll learn about key dates, program leadership, community guidelines, and how to engage with your peers.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this module, you will be able to:

- Identify important dates and deadlines for the program
- Understand Penn Engineering's history and the Computer and Information Science department
- Meet key faculty and student support staff
- Describe community guidelines and values
- Use Slack as your primary communication tool
- Explore engagement opportunities and get involved
