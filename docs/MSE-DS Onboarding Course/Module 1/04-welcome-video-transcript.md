## Key Points

- MSE-DS is a highly selective program with rigorous coursework
- Faculty are leaders in research across multiple fields of computer science
- Core topics: artificial intelligence, big data systems, deep learning, natural language processing, Internet and web systems, machine learning
- Coursework may be challenging at first, but support is available from faculty, staff, and classmates
- You're joining a community of problem solvers and creative thinkers
- The online learning team is committed to your success
- Feedback and engagement from students are valued and encouraged
