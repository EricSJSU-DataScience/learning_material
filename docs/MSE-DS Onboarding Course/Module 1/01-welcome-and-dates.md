# MSE-DS Welcome! Important Onboarding Dates

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of these nine modules, you will be able to:

- Describe how the program works, including associated requirements, costs, and the registration process
- Demonstrate confidence navigating the Canvas platform and various online tools used in your courses
- Describe University policies such as academic integrity
- Identify campus services available to you
