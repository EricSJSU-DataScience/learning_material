# Meet the Student Success Team

The online learning team at Penn Engineering is here to support you every step of the way. Our dedicated staff members are committed to your success and creating a vibrant, welcoming community.

## Academic Advising Team
![alt text](08_image2.png)
**Adrienne Yoder** (she/her)  
*Associate Director of Academic Advising for Penn Engineering Online*

Adrienne believes deeply in making high-quality education accessible to all students through remote learning, but also recognizes that being an online student is challenging, especially in making organic connections. Her passion lies in engaging the online community and creating opportunities for them to support each other and develop lasting relationships well after they've graduated.
